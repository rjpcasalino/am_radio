Logo Design for am_radio
========================

Concept: Clean, minimalist vintage radio icon using Unicode/graphical design

The logo uses a simple graphical representation of a vintage radio:

Design Elements:
- Antenna with circular tip at top
- Rounded rectangle radio body (vintage style)
- Central tuning dial with pointer
- "AM" text in bold
- Horizontal speaker grille lines at bottom

Color Scheme:
- Background: #1A0F00 (dark brown - bakelite)
- Foreground: #E8A020 (amber - vintage glow)

This design is clean, recognizable at small sizes, and matches
the app's vintage bakelite aesthetic. The minimalist approach
ensures clarity on all screen densities and device displays.

Technical Implementation:
- Primary: Unicode radio emoji (📻 U+1F4FB) rendered in amber
- Fallback: Graphical vector-style radio drawn with PIL
- Both approaches maintain the vintage color palette
- Scales cleanly from 48x48 (mdpi) to 192x192 (xxxhdpi)
